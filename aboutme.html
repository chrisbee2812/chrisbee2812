CHRIS BICKNELL

All about me

MY CHILDHOOD YEARS

My life as a child, January 16th, 2025

Born, late December 1972, Pembroke Dock Hospital, Pembrokshire, Wales.

So, the big question, do I consider my self as being Welsh? Deep down, and seeing as I was born in Wales, I suppose I have to. However, I feel most Welsh would laugh at me even contemplating the possibility of me being Welsh.

Having moved from Wales, to England at the age of 2 years, and only ever having been back to Wales a handful of times to visit, on the surface I'm English, or better still, British.
