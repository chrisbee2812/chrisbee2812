Discworld Random Sentence Generator

Click the button to generate a random Discworld sentence!

I would say that I'm a fan of the late Terry Pratchett and his Discworld novels. However, to this day I have still not read all of the books! Those I have read are wonderful, and I would highly recommend them to anyone, whether or not a fan of fantasy novels.

The original remit for this project was to create a random sentence generator of our choosing. My first itteration, which completed the project consisted of a possible 1000 different sentences. Just 10 possible characters, with 10 possible actions in 10 possible locations. Today there are over 3.5 million possible sentences, including having a possible colleague (2 in 3 sentences will have such), or whether it is in the future, past or currently occuring.
